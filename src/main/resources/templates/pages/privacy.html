Privacy policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This policy applies to GameVaultr at https://gamevaultr.com/. It describes how I handle information when you use the site. Contact me at clydeconor@gmail.com.

What is collected

  • Account. Username and a hashed password only (no plain text passwords; no email for sign-up in the current product).
  • Your content. What you add in the app—collection, play logs, reviews, playthroughs, notes, and similar—stored with your account.
  • IGDB. Public game catalog data (titles, art, etc.) from IGDB may be cached in my database; that is general catalog info, not a profile “about” you. Search queries go to IGDB to return results. A Twitch API token for IGDB lives on the server (app credentials)—that is not your personal Twitch login.
  • Session & device. A server session keeps you signed in and can remember in-app choices (e.g. search filters) during your visit. Session and CSRF cookies are strictly necessary for login and secure forms. The collection page may store optional UI preferences in localStorage on your device. I do not add advertising or analytics trackers in the shipped app; fonts/icons may load from a public CDN and game art from IGDB as described.

Use, storage, and sharing

I use this data only to run GameVaultr (accounts, collection, search, reliability, security). Your account and library data are stored in PostgreSQL on Neon (neon.tech). Neon and other providers may keep backups or replicas under their own terms—I do not run a separate personal backup or export routine beyond relying on that infrastructure. Hosting may still produce routine operational logs; I do not use logs to advertise or to track you across sites. I send search/metadata requests to IGDB as needed. I do not sell your personal information. I may disclose information if the law requires it or to protect the service, users, or others.

Your rights & deletion

You may have rights (depending on where you live) to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your information. Self-serve tools may not exist in every deployment. To request access, correction, or deletion of your account and data, email me at the address above. I retain your data while your account exists and only as needed to run the service; when you ask for deletion, I delete or anonymize it subject to legal requirements and how long copies may persist in infrastructure backups outside my direct control.

Security & children

Passwords are hashed. Use HTTPS in production. No storage or transmission is perfect. GameVaultr is not aimed at children under 13; I do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you think I have, contact me and I will delete it.
